Last week, I’ve attended [Focus Wales](https://focuswales.com/), a big music/film festival in Wrexham (Wales)!

I’ve watched lots of fantastic short films: 3 documentaries, 4 thrillers, and the amazing [Flock](https://letterboxd.com/film/flock-2025/).

And I saw these bands in concert:

- Campfire Social
- Wrkhouse
- Slate
- Idlewild
- [Lawn Chair](https://lawnchairband.com/)
- [Bitterfly](https://www.instagram.com/bitterflyband/)
- [Shame](https://www.shame.world/)
- [Baby Brave](https://babybrave.bandcamp.com/)
- [Internet Fatigue](https://www.instagram.com/internet.fatigue/)
- Gans
- [Alpha Male Tea Party](https://alphamaleteaparty.bandcamp.com)

I’ve known quite a few of these bands already – and I was especially happy to have finally witnessed the amazing Alpha Male Tea Party live, after being a fan for many years. There were also some bands I’ve seen for the first time: Slate, Lawn Chair, and Bitterfly were all great – and the latter two played in a [nice little pub](https://maps.app.goo.gl/iLGzS34emgwQ7yUb9), which made the experience extra fun.

Needless to say, it’s been a great time! If you happen to be in or near Wales next spring, you should check it out. Meanwhile, I’m already looking forward to the next festival (which might be [Vegan Camp Out](https://www.vegancampout.co.uk/) or [Green Man](https://www.greenman.net/))!
